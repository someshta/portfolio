About Me

In 2012, as I was pursuing a degree in apparel design, I took a required course on web development. My initial thought was "Why do I need to do this?" In no time at all Coding 101 became my favorite class, thus beginning my journey into the world of web development. After graduation I quickly realized the fashion industry wasn't right for me, but I never lost my love for design. In late 2017 I decided to sign up for UC Davis's coding bootcamp, where I could rekindle that HTML flame and put my design skills to use once again. Now I'm a junior developer, in love with what I do, ready to share my sparkle with the world.
